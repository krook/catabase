Welcome.

Project Catabase is a massive corpus of cat pictures and videos encoded and compiled by the cats themselves using Formula Translation technology and the IBM Feline Thinking methodology.

We think you'll agree it's a revolutionary way to renew one of humankind's most valuable natural resources by employing the resource to renew itself in perpetuity.

In this repository you'll find the corpus of cata itself, as well as instructions on how to feed, read, and analyze the data using the NoSqueakL tools.
